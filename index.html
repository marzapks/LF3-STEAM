Live from Third Street STEAM Framework

Live from Third Street is a community-based creative technology and music education program where students learn by building, listening, experimenting, performing, and reflecting.

S

Science

Sound waves, vibration, frequency, resonance, acoustics, microphone behavior, and how outdoor spaces affect live music.

T

Technology

MIDI, Logic on iPad, custom apps, audio-reactive visuals, live video effects, 360 camera capture, and Bluetooth keyboard performance control.

E

Engineering

Live sound setup, signal flow, drum miking, cable routing, mixer operation, speaker placement, power planning, and troubleshooting.

A

Arts

Improvisation, feel, harmony, rhythm, composition, visual design, storytelling, performance, and cultural/community expression.

M

Math

BPM, subdivisions, measures, looping, chord structure, intervals, patterns, form, timing, and rhythm-based problem solving.

About the program

Live from Third Street is a service-rooted community music program where real performance experience becomes a hands-on learning environment.

What happens in the setup?

Mario sets up piano, MIDI keyboard, Logic on iPad, custom apps, sound textures, QSC speakers, mixer routing, 360 camera capture, and direct audio recording. Deon sets up the drum kit, chooses cymbals, shapes the kit orientation, and helps build the full live environment.

What happens in the session?

Mario and Deon specialize in improvisational music creation, often entering the session without knowing exactly what will happen next. Students learn how trust, rhythm, harmony, listening, intuition, and technology help new music emerge in real time.

What happens after?

The captured 360 video and direct mixer audio become a media-production lesson. Students learn how sessions are edited, organized, presented, shared, and turned into community storytelling.
